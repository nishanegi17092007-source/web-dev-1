Bears are large, powerful mammals that belong to the family Ursidae. They are classified as caniforms and are found throughout North America, South America, Europe, and Asia. There are several species of bears, including the polar bear, brown bear, black bear, Asiatic black bear, sun bear, spectacled bear, and sloth bear. They have long shaggy hair, rudimentary tails, and plantigrade feet.
